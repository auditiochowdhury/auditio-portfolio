About Me

I'm a passionate 22-year-old Bengali American who had the privilege of growing up in the vibrant city of New York. From a young age, I was captivated by the world of technology, and that fascination evolved into a deep love for coding. I believe that programming is a language that allows us to build bridges between ideas and transform them into tangible solutions. It empowers us to shape the future, and I am honored to be a part of this ever-evolving digital landscape.

Throughout my journey, I've acquired a diverse set of skills and expertise in various programming languages, frameworks, and technologies. I constantly challenge myself to learn and adapt, keeping up with the latest industry trends to stay at the forefront of innovation. But it's not just about the code. I firmly believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork. I thrive in an environment where ideas are shared, and collective efforts bring about extraordinary results. Building meaningful connections with fellow developers and clients is something I value greatly.

When I'm not immersed in lines of code, you can find me exploring the city's vibrant cultural scene, indulging in various cuisines, or capturing moments through my camera lens.
